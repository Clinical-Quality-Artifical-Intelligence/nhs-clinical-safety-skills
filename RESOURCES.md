## Careers as a DCSO

Nurses increasingly take on DCSO responsibilities as health IT becomes central to care delivery. To develop in this role:

1. **Complete the e-LfH clinical risk management modules** (free, NHS-endorsed)
2. **Familiarise yourself with DCB0129/DCB0160** — read the actual standards
3. **Get experience reviewing health IT tools** — use the skills in this repo to practise
4. **Connect with the NHS Clinical Safety community** — NHS Futures platform, RCN Digital Health forum
5. **Consider formal training** — NHS England run training sessions for new DCSOs (contact clinical.safety@nhs.net)
