## Mashoko eKutanga / Introduction

Duramazwi rino rakagadzirirwa vacheza vanoubva kuZimbabwe vari kushanda muNHS yeUnited
Kingdom. Shona yechirimo (standard Shona / ChiShona) yakashandiswa mutsinhiridzano dzose
kuitira kuti mazwi zvinzwisisike zvakanaka.

Zvipimo zvakadai DCB0129 neDCB0160 zviri zvipimo zveUK — mifungo (acronyms) inoramba iri
muChirungu, asi tsanangudzo dzakapiwa muChiShona.

## Mazwi Akakosha / Core Terms

---

**English:** Digital Clinical Safety Officer (DCSO)
**ChiShona:** Digital Clinical Safety Officer (DCSO) — Mukuru weKuchengeteka kweUrapi hweDhijitari

> DCSO munhu mukati meNHS anoshanda kuona kuti masisitimu eDhijitari eUtano akachengeteka
> kuvaguri. Semunhu anochenjera (nurse), unogona kushanda neDCSO yako kana sirisitemu itsva
> yeDhijitari ichipinzwa muwadhi kana dhipatimendi yako.

---

**English:** Hazard
**ChiShona:** Njodzi / Chinhu Chinokonzera Njodzi (Hazard)

> Mune zvokuchengeteka kweUrapi, *njodzi* ndiwo mamiriro kana chinhu chinogona kukonzera
> kukuvara kumusiki kana kuvashandi veUtano. Muenzaniso: chitikiti chakaora pamusoro pemuriwo,
> kana chipiganiso chemutsa chakakuvara.

---

**English:** Risk
**ChiShona:** Njodzi yeMagomana (Risk) / Imba yeMagomana

> *Risk* kubatanidza kwemakwikwi ekuti chinhu chakaipa chichaitika pamwe nekuoma kwekukuvara
> kunogona kuitika. Inosiyana ne *hazard* — hazard ndicho chikonzero chenjodzi, nepo risk
> iri kutarisisa kwemakwikwi uye zvacho zvinogona kuitika.

---

**English:** Clinical safety
**ChiShona:** Kuchengeteka kweUrapi (Clinical Safety)

> *Kuchengeteka kweUrapi* kunotaura nezvenhanho dzose, maitiro, uye masisitimu anochengeta
> vaguri kubva panjodzi kana kukanganisa kunogona kuitika munzvimbo yekurapwa. Ichi chidimbu
> chikuru chebasa revashandi vose veUtano, kusanganisira vacheza.

---

**English:** Hazard log
**ChiShona:** Buku reNjodzi (Hazard Log)

> *Buku renjodzi* ibhuku reofisi rinorekodhwesa njodzi dzose dzakawanikwa munesisitimu
> reUtano kana maitiro eurapi. Rinosanganisira mwero wenjodzi imwe neimwe, nhanho
> dzekudzora, uye mamiriro enjodzi imwe neimwe. Ichi chinodiwa pasi peDCB0129 neDCB0160.

---

**English:** Risk assessment
**ChiShona:** Ongororo yeNjodzi (Risk Assessment)

> *Ongororo yenjodzi* inzira yakanyatsorongwa yekuziva, kuongorora, uye kuongorora njodzi
> kuti uzive mwero wayo, woburikidza kuona nhanho dzinodiwa kuderedza njodzi. Semunhu
> anochenjera, unogona kukumbirwa kupinda muongororo yenjodzi inobatana newadhi yako kana
> zvishandiso zvaunoshanda nazvo.

---

**English:** Clinical Safety Case Report (CSCR)
**ChiShona:** Clinical Safety Case Report (CSCR) — Ripooti yeKuchengeteka kweUrapi

> CSCR igwaro rakakosha rinopupura kuti sirisitemu yeDhijitari reUtano yakakodzera kushandiswa
> munzvimbo yeurapi. DCB0129 inorisunga kune vakagadzira masisitimu eurapi, uye DCB0160
> inorisunga kune vanosarasara masisitimu aya muNHS. Ichi chiratidzo chekuti njodzi dzose
> dzakajairika dzakaongorowa uye dziri kukumidzwa zvakakwana.

---

**English:** Clinical Risk Management Plan (CRMP)
**ChiShona:** Clinical Risk Management Plan (CRMP) — Chirongwa chekuKumidzwa kweNjodzi dzeUrapi

> CRMP igwaro rinorondedza kuti njodzi dzeurapi dzinoziviwa sei, dziongororwa sei, uye
> dzikumidzwa sei muzviitiko zvose zveupenyu hwesirisitemu yeDhijitari reUtano. Ichi
> chinodiwa zvikuru paDCB0129 kune vakagadzira uye DCB0160 kune vanosarasara.

---

**English:** Manufacturer (of health IT)
**ChiShona:** Mugadziri (weIT yeUtano) — Kambani kana Sangano Rinogadzira Masisitimu eDhijitari eUtano

> Mune zveDCB0129, *mugadziri* ikambani kana sangano rinogadzira uye kutengesa sirisitemu
> yeDhijitari reUtano (semuenzaniso Electronic Patient Record kana chipiganiso chemutsa
> chine software). Vane basa guru rekuchengeteka kwesirisitemu vasati varitengesa.

---

**English:** Deployer (of health IT)
**ChiShona:** Musaradziri (weIT yeUtano) — Sangano Rinosarasara Sirisitemu yeDhijitari

> Mune zveDCB0160, *musaradziri* isangano reUtano (semuenzaniso NHS Trust) rinosarasara
> uye kushanda nesirisitemu yeDhijitari reUtano. Vane basa rekusarasara zvakachengeteka,
> kudzidzisa vashandi, uye kukumidzwa kwenjodzi kunoramba kuchitika munzvimbo yavo.

---

**English:** Acceptable risk
**ChiShona:** Njodzi Inogashirwa (Acceptable Risk)

> *Njodzi inogashirwa* imwero wenjodzi unoonekwa kuderedza zvakakwana kuitira kuti hapana
> nhanho dzimwe dzekunyatsozvibata dzinodiwa. Haikurevei hapana njodzi — inoreva kuti
> njodzi yacho yakaongorowa uye yakaonekwa kuderedza zvakakwana maererano nezvinobvuma
> nyanzvi.

---

**English:** Tolerable risk
**ChiShona:** Njodzi Inobwirwa (Tolerable Risk)

> *Njodzi inobwirwa* imwero wenjodzi usiri panzvimbo yakagashirwa zvachose asi inogashirwa
> semutauro nekuti zvakanakira sirisitemu reUtano zvakawanda kupfuura njodzi. Nhanho
> dzichiri dzinodiwa kuderedza njodzi chero kwazvinobvuma (maitiro eALARP — As Low As
> Reasonably Practicable).

---

**English:** Unacceptable risk
**ChiShona:** Njodzi Isinogashirwa (Unacceptable Risk)

> *Njodzi isinogashirwa* imwero wenjodzi wakakura zvakanyanya kubvumidza sirisitemu kana
> maitiro kuenderera mberi pasina nhanho dzimwe dzekunyatsozvibata. Munzira yakadaro,
> sirisitemu haigatiki kana inofanira kumira kusvika njodzi yatoorerwa kumwero unogashirwa.

---

**English:** Severity
**ChiShona:** Kuoma / Mwero weKukuvara (Severity)

> *Kuoma* kunoyera kukuvara kunogona kukonzerwa nenjodzi kumuguri kana kumushandi. Kazhinji
> kunoratwirwa kubva pachidiki (minor) kusvika kukuru kwazvo (catastrophic). Semunhu
> anochenjera, kunzwisisa kuoma kunokosha pakupepeta zviitiko uye kuongorora njodzi
> panzvimbo pako pebasa.

---

**English:** Likelihood
**ChiShona:** Makwikwi / Zvimhanyiso Zvekuitika (Likelihood)

> *Makwikwi* anonga kazhinji sei kana makwikwi ekuti chinhu chenjodzi chichaitika.
> Pamwe nekuoma, izi ndizvo zvidimbu zviviri zvikuru zveongororo yenjodzi. Makwikwi
> makuru pamwe nekuoma kukuru kunoratidza mwero mukuru wenjodzi unoda kutariswa nekukurumidza.

---

**English:** Risk control
**ChiShona:** Kudzora Njodzi (Risk Control)

> *Kudzora njodzi* kunotaura nezvenhanho dzose dzinobatwa kuderedza makwikwi kana kuoma
> kwenjodzi. Ichi chinosanganisira kudzidziswa, mararamiro ekuona, manzwi esirisitemu,
> uye shanduko mumaitiro eurapi. Vacheza kazhinji vari munzira yekutanga mukushandisa
> nhanho dzekudzora njodzi.

---

**English:** Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA)
**ChiShona:** Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA) — Ongororo yeInzira Dzekukundikana kweSisitimu nezvaZvinokonzerwa

> FMEA inzira yakanyatsorongwa yekuongorora inzira dzinogona kusisitimu kana maitiro
> kukundikana uye zvakonzerwa zvakagoneka zvezvokukundikana izvozvo pakuchengeteka
> kwemuguri. Inoshandiswa kuziva uye kudzivirira zviitiko zvagona kuitika vasati zvaitika.
> Unogona kukumbirwa kupinda muFMEA semunhu ane ruzivo mumaitiro eurapi.

---

**English:** Patient safety
**ChiShona:** Kuchengeteka kweMuguri (Patient Safety)

> *Kuchengeteka kwemuguri* chinangwa chikuru chemasisitimu ose eUtano — kudzivirira
> kukanganisa kwakaipa kana zviitiko zvingakuvadza vaguri. Ichi chikamu chikuru chepasirose
> cheWHO neNHS, uye chidziro chebasa rese rekuchengeteka kweUrapi.

---

**English:** Clinical judgment
**ChiShona:** Pfungwa dzeUrapi / Kutarisira kweNyanzvi yeUrapi (Clinical Judgment)

> *Pfungwa dzeurapi* uchakara wemushandi weUtano kushandisa ruzivo, ruzivo rwakawana, uye
> kufunga zvakadzama kuita zvisarudzo zvakangwara nezverurimi rwemuguri. Kunyange sirisitemu
> rakakwirira sei reDhijitari harigatiri kupomera pfungwa dzeurapi dzomuchenjeri wakadzidza.

---

**English:** Health IT system
**ChiShona:** Sirisitemu yeIT yeUtano / Sirisitemu yeDhijitari reUtano (Health IT System)

> *Sirisitemu yeIT yeUtano* inotaura nezvegatsi imwe netechnorogii yeDhijitari kana
> software inoshandiswa pakupa huduma dzeUtano. Inosanganisira Rekodhisi dzeVaguri
> dzeDhijitari (EPR), masisitimu ekuodha mishonga, masisitimu edzidzo yemifananidzo
> yeDhijitari, uye zvimwe zvishandiso zveDhijitari zvinowandisa maitiro eurapi. Masisitimu
> ose eIT eUtano anoshandiswa muNHS anofanira kuteerera DCB0129 neDCB0160.

### Zvipimo zveUK Zvakataururwa / UK Standards Referenced

- **DCB0129** — Chipimo cheUK kune *vagadziri* vemasisitimu eIT eUtano: kuona kuti
  zvigadzirwa zveDhijitari zvakachengeteka vasati vatengesa kana kuapa NHS.
- **DCB0160** — Chipimo cheUK kune *vanosarasara* masisitimu eIT eUtano: kuona kuti
  masangano eNHS anosarasara uye anotora masisitimu eDhijitari zvakachengeteka.
